Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. This Privacy Policy informs you about the nature, scope, and purpose of the collection and use of personal data on our website.

1. Data Collection

We collect data when you use our services, including:

  • IP address and browser type (automatically)
  • Contact data (if submitted via forms)
  • Usage data for analytics (e.g., visited pages, clicks)

2. Use of Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of improving our service, securing the website, and (if applicable) responding to inquiries. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your consent.

3. Analytics

We may use tools like Google Analytics or Plausible Analytics to anonymously evaluate user behavior. Your IP address is anonymized before processing.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to ensure technical functionality and improve user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

Jane Green - privacy@co2aware.org

7. Hosting & Server Logs

Our site is hosted by a European provider. Server logs are stored for security purposes and deleted after 14 days.
